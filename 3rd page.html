crocodiles

The saltwater crocodile (\(Crocodylusporosus\)) is a reptile that lives in the Sundarbans, a delta in India and Bangladesh. It is the largest living reptile in the world, and is a top predator in the Sundarbans ecosystem.
Male saltwater crocodiles can grow up to 20 feet long and weigh more than 1,000 kg
